Installing a Minecraft Resource Pack

Step 1: Download and Install the Resource Pack

Once you have the resource pack you'd like downloaded to your computer, double-click the file and it will automatically install itself to your Windows 10 edition of Minecraft.

Step 2: Open Minecraft and Create a New World

Your Windows 10 Edition of the game should now be open. Hit 'Play' on the main menu, then under the worlds tab, hit Create New and Create New World. Choose your preferred settings and name your world. Then in the bottom left, click Resource Packs.

Step 3: Activate the Resource Pack

Under My Packs, select the pack you want on your server and click Activate. You can see if it's applied properly in the top right under Active. Once you know it's applied, hit Create to start your world.

Step 4: Save and Quit

You'll be loaded into your world with the resource pack enabled. Press Escape, then click Save and Quit to return to your worlds menu. You can now close the game or minimize the window.

Step 5: Upload to GBNODES Server

If you haven't already generated your GBNODES server world, make sure your server is set to the Bedrock version you want. Generate the world, close the server, and upload the world.
